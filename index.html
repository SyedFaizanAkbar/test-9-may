    About The Author

    Kate Clary has worked in an elementary school setting for most of her working life. An avid book reader
    since she was young child, she has now authored fun, short chapter books for children between the
    ages of 4-9. Kate is an animal lover and lives with her two rescue cats. She also enjoys traveling to
    places near and far. Curling up with a good book, with her cats by her side, is still one of her favorite
    past times.
